### What I promise as a manager
1. I'll be reasonable and reasonably kind for times when you are not a 100%.\
2. I'll give you a clear line of logic why I disagree, and if you really want to know, what I think should be done.\
3. I'll take care of things that are not your core work.\
4. I'll be your voice where its clear you are not at fault.\
5. I'll show respect by being polite, appreciative in public, and critical in private.\
6. If I find you are not able to stay in the team and its not something you can help, I'll help you move.

### Non-negotiable behavioral traits for both me and you
1. Be logical, think through actions before you execute. Have reasoning that you can explain.\
2. Express your thought process, but remember to be kind and polite. Both are equally critical.\
3. Be honest and sincere. No making up stories and covering up, then make cover ups for this cover up.\
4. Be upfront and unafraid of mistakes. Own up to errors, and wihtout freaking out, think of ways to fix the current mess and prevent a future mess.\

My promise stays till your promise stays.
